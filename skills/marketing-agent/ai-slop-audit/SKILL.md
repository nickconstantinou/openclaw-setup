# SKILL: Elite AI Sloppiness Auditor & Autonomous Rewrite Engine

---

# Purpose

Detect AI-generated sloppiness and iteratively transform content into **elite professional-level writing**, equivalent to a **30+ year veteran author or copywriter**.

---

# Core Scoring

**0.00–1.00 scale** with detailed dimensions:

| Score | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| 0.00–0.20 | Generic AI slop |
| 0.21–0.40 | Weak marketing copy |
| 0.41–0.60 | Average writing |
| 0.61–0.80 | Professional-level |
| 0.81–0.94 | Expert writing |
| 0.95–1.00 | Elite professional writing |

**Target:** ≥ 0.95

---

# Evaluation Dimensions

1. **Specificity** – concrete examples, quantification, measurable claims
2. **Insight Density** – non-obvious observations, expert-level reasoning
3. **Structural Quality** – logical flow, hierarchy, narrative progression
4. **Language Precision** – tight, concise, strong verbs, no filler
5. **Persuasive Strength** – authority, confidence, reader benefit clarity
6. **Voice Authenticity** – human tone, personality, natural cadence
7. **Human Rhythm Markers** – sentence variation, pacing, readability
8. **Semantic Originality** – unique phrasing, non-template ideas
9. **Token Density** – information-rich content per word
10. **Narrative Coherence** – internal consistency and clarity of argument
11. **AI Fingerprint Detection** – repeated patterns, symmetry, robotic phrasing
12. **Burstiness Analysis** – natural variation in sentence length and rhythm
13. **Persona Matching** – aligns with intended authorial voice

---

# AI Sloppiness Signals

Penalize:

- Template language ("In today's fast-paced world", "Additionally", etc.)
- Predictable formatting (uniform paragraphs, symmetrical lists)
- Hedging, passive voice, weak recommendations
- Filler, repetition, inflated wording
- Generic motivational fluff, buzzwords, overused transitions
